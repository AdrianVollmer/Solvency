About Market Data

  • Market data is fetched from Yahoo Finance for symbols with active positions
  • Data is fetched for the date range from your first activity to today
  • Only daily closing prices are stored (one data point per trading day)
  • Gaps of up to 5 days are normal (weekends and holidays)
  • Status indicates: Complete (data within last 5 days), Stale (no recent data), No data (not yet fetched)
